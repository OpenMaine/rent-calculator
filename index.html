Welcome to the Portland Allowable Rent Increase Calculator

In November 2020, Portland passed a new ordinance to limit increases in rent. The purpose of the ordinance is to promote community stability and protect tenants, while ensuring landlords receive a fair return on investment.

Landlords and tenants can use this tool to calculate their unit’s allowable rent increase.

Full text of the ordinance can be found here. It is Article XII, which covers sections 6-230 to 6-243. The City of Portland has a list of frequently asked questions (FAQ) about the ordinance here.

Landlords Are Required to Give Notice:

Landlords are required to give at least 75 days advance notice of an increase in rent, by sending a signed document to the tenant. The letter must include the date tenancy began, date of rent increase, and justifications for increase. See section 6.234-d.
